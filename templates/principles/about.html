The 11 Principles

In October 2009, 130 community members came together and voted on 11 core community principles. Collectively, these represent our priorities for the Green Line Extension. CCP along with hundreds of Somerville community members have been working to move these principles forward into action.

There are currently four working groups and a resident advisory team that are working to ensure the communities principles are carried out.
